About

Hey there, I'm Armando, a skilled and versatile video editor with six years of self-taught experience. I specialize in creating engaging content that captures viewers' attention. Proficient in Adobe Premiere Pro, Adobe Photoshop, and Canva, I can also design YouTube thumbnails, logos, and banners.

Throughout my career, I've worked on a wide range of projects, including educational content, travel videos, vlogs, memory collages, and content for children. My editing style is adaptable, whether it involves adding creative elements like green-screen effects, sound effects, and text animation, or keeping it simple with jump cuts and smooth transitions.

I take pride in my attention to detail and efficiency. I genuinely enjoy the editing process, from selecting the perfect music to incorporating captivating graphics and transitions. By understanding clients' needs, I can transform raw footage into a polished final product. If you're seeking a video editor who combines skill, creativity, and effective communication, I'm here to bring your vision to life.
